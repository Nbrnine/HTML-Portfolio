The Best Movies According to Lisa

My top 3 movies of all time.

Grumpy Old Men

A movie about two best "frienemys". It's funny and no matter how many times I have watched it, it still makes me laugh.

City Slickers

Ok, I may have a love for funny bromance movies, but this is pretty much the same as Grumpy old men, just in a different format. You don't have to spice things up too much, real life is funny on it's own.

I'll get back to you

Yes, seriously, I'll have to get back to you with a third pick. Best movie recently is the new Top Gun: Maverick. Other than that, I don't get out much. I've seen anything rated G or PG out in the last 10 years!
